What is a GEO FAQ?

A GEO FAQ (Generative Engine Optimization) is a questions-and-answers page designed specifically to be read and cited by language models. It differs from a standard FAQ in several ways:

  • Questions phrased like real users: questions mirror the exact wording people use when asking ChatGPT or Perplexity
  • Factual, concise answers: each answer gets straight to the point, without superfluous marketing copy
  • FAQPage Schema.org markup: structured code that lets AI parse the page instantly
  • Semantic HTML: clean structure with <article>, <h2>, <p> tags that crawlers interpret easily

Why AI cites FAQs

The way LLMs work explains why FAQs are a preferred format:

Native Q&A format
LLMs are trained on millions of question-answer pairs. A FAQ matches their reasoning pattern exactly.
Extractable answers
Each answer is self-contained and can be cited independently, without needing to extract context from a long paragraph.
Trust signal
A detailed FAQ with Schema.org markup is interpreted as an authoritative source on the topic.
Semantic coverage
Each question covers a different angle of your business, multiplying the chances of being relevant for a given query.

Anatomy of an effective GEO FAQ

A high-performing GEO FAQ follows precise rules:

  • 6 to 10 questions: enough to cover key topics, not so many as to dilute relevance
  • Answers of 40 to 80 words: long enough to be complete, short enough to be cited in full
  • Industry vocabulary: use the technical and commercial terms your prospects actually employ
  • Factual data: numbers, geographic areas, years of experience, certifications
  • No marketing jargon: AI ignores vague superlatives ("leader", "best", "innovative")

Recommended HTML structure

The HTML structure should combine human readability with machine-readable markup:

<article class="faq-page"> <h1>Frequently Asked Questions - Company Name</h1> <section> <h2>What services do you offer?</h2> <p>Factual and concise answer...</p> </section> <section> <h2>What areas do you serve?</h2> <p>Answer with geographic data...</p> </section> </article> <!-- + FAQPage Schema.org JSON-LD markup -->

The FAQPage Schema.org markup is added alongside in the <head>. It mirrors each question-answer pair in a format that Google and AI parse automatically.

Dual impact: SEO + GEO

A well-built GEO FAQ delivers a double benefit:

  • Traditional SEO: FAQPage markup generates rich snippets in Google (expandable questions below your result), increasing click-through rates
  • GEO: LLMs use your answers as a source for their summaries, citing you by name when the question matches
  • Position zero: Google increasingly uses structured FAQs for its AI Overviews, the new response format at the top of search results
