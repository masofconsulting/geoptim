GEO is the equivalent of SEO for generative AI engines. While SEO optimizes a site to be found on Google, GEO optimizes a site to be cited by AI assistants: ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini, Grok and Mistral Le Chat.

In 2026, one in three searches goes directly through an AI assistant, bypassing Google entirely. Sites not optimized for GEO are simply absent from these responses.
