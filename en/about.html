Geoptim is a SaaS tool for GEO audit and optimization. Our mission: enable every business to be properly indexed, understood and cited by ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini, Grok, Mistral and Copilot.

Our mission

Since 2024, generative AI assistants have fundamentally changed the way people search for information. One in three searches no longer goes through Google; it goes directly through an AI assistant that synthesizes an answer.

The problem: most websites are not prepared for this reality. A robots.txt that blocks AI crawlers, no llms.txt file, missing or incomplete Schema.org markup. The site becomes invisible to an entire generation of AI.

Geoptim solves this problem in minutes. Free analysis, AI-generated files, immediately deployable on any CMS.
